My Bio

Hi there! My name is Teagan Algarra, this is my bio about me! I am a current freshman at Concordia University Irvine. I'm from Albuquerque, New Mexico and was recruited to play beach volleyball. I've been playing volleyball for about 12 years, and it's one of my biggest passions. I'm also an artist who loves to paint and be creative in everything I do! In my freetime, I love to lift weights, read, play video games, and listen to music. My Spotify says my most listened to genres are rock, indie, and RnB. Some of my favorite music artists are JCole, Frank Ocean, Arctic Monkeys, Mac Miller, and Twenty One Pilots. My family's nickname for me is "Pie", and at home I have one black cat named Toad, and a chow chow puppy named Mika. I love horror films, but my favorite iconic movie is either "Pitch Perfect" or "Napoleon Dynamite". My biggest comfort food is Kraft Mac n Cheese.
